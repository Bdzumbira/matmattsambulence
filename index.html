Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service?

We provide nationwide coverage with our base in Chiredzi, Zimbabwe. Our services extend to all major cities and rural areas, ensuring comprehensive medical transport coverage throughout the country.

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

Our response time varies based on location, but we aim to reach urban areas within 15-30 minutes and rural areas within 30-60 minutes. For subscribers, we provide priority response times. Our 24/7 dispatch center ensures immediate response to all emergency calls.

What types of medical transport do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of services including:

  • Emergency medical transport
  • Inter-hospital transfers
  • Medical escort services
  • Non-emergency medical transport
  • Special events medical coverage
  • Long-distance medical transport

How does the subscription service work?

Our subscription service provides affordable emergency medical coverage based on your location:

Coverage Options:

  • Local (Within 15KM Radius)
    • Basic: $1/month - Transport with Technician
    • Standard: $2/month - Transport + Medical Accessories
    • Premium: $3/month - Full Service with Life Support
  • Out of Town (Beyond 15KM)
    • Basic: $2/month - Transport with Technician
    • Standard: $3/month - Transport + Medical Accessories
    • Premium: $4/month - Full Service with Life Support

Key Benefits:

  • Priority emergency response
  • Family coverage included
  • 24/7 medical advice
  • Discounted non-emergency services

Getting Started: Select your area → Choose a plan → Pay via EcoCash → Submit proof → Instant activation

What medical equipment is available in your ambulances?

Our ambulances are fully equipped with:

  • Advanced life support equipment
  • Cardiac monitors and defibrillators
  • Oxygen therapy systems
  • Emergency medication supplies
  • Patient monitoring devices
  • Specialized transport equipment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including:

  • Major credit and debit cards
  • Mobile money transfers
  • Bank transfers
  • Cash payments
  • Medical insurance (for covered services)

For emergency services, payment can be arranged after the service is provided.

What should I do while waiting for the ambulance?

While waiting for our ambulance:

  • Stay calm and reassure the patient
  • Keep the patient still unless they're in immediate danger
  • Clear a path for the ambulance crew
  • Gather any relevant medical documents
  • Have someone ready to direct the crew to the patient
  • Follow any instructions given by our emergency dispatch team

Are your medical staff certified?

Yes, all our medical staff are fully certified and experienced professionals. Our team includes:

  • Licensed paramedics
  • Emergency Medical Technicians (EMTs)
  • Registered nurses
  • Emergency care specialists

Our staff regularly undergo training and certification updates to maintain the highest standards of care.
